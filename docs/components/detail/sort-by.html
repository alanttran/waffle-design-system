Accessibility

  • ARIA “role=form” on a <form> turns the widget into a region making it navigable in screen readers using shortcuts.
  • The <form> contains a single <fieldset> to group radio options under the label “Sort by” followed by the submit button.
    • When <input> is focused, the <legend> content (“Sort by…”) is announced followed by the <input>’s<label> (“…most recent”).
    • Full phrase when focused on first option: “Sort by most recent, selected, radio button, one of four.”
      • “one out of four” is the count based on matching “name = sort-method”
